Failure modes — what kills multibagger theses

Liquidity trap
SME-IPO names like C2C Advanced can have wide spreads. Use limit orders. Avoid market orders > 0.5 % of avg daily volume.
Dilution shock
Cash-burning US names (Palladyne, AirJoule, Larimar) will likely raise equity. Model 20-40 % dilution in 24 months.
Order lumpiness
Defense / rail orders move quarterly results by ±50 %. Don't react to single-quarter misses if the order book is intact.
Binary FDA risk
Larimar's BLA in Jun 2026 = single binary event. Either +200 % or -60 %. Size accordingly.
Regulator U-turn
C2C had a SEBI audit-system flag pre-listing. SME-segment regulatory risk is asymmetric vs. mainboard.
Pivot un-proof
Duos & Premier Explosives pivots are nascent. If new revenue stream contributes < 30 % in 4 quarters, exit.
